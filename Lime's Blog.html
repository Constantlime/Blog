Hello, Once again.

5th-December-2022, Monday

Name's Lime

My father was invited to work on a project at the University of Alberta a few months ago. I don't know anything else, but I know we've been packing and preparing to leave. In fact, we're leaving for Canada tommorow.

I was born in Canada, lived there for eight years, and have memories there, so I have some experience. But leaving Pakistan will be difficult because I have the most loyal and valuable friends, and I am the best student in the section. We call ourselves "3 Weird Friends" and leaving them will be heartbreaking. We've had the best time ever, and replacing each other will be impossible. I can't forget the laughter and the trips.

But as Joseph Cambell once said:

"We must let go of the life we planned,
so as to accept the one that is waiting for us".

The Journey

7th-December-2022, Wednesday

The journey took approximately 30 hours. I couldn't write before (duh), so I'm writing today.

The day started out pretty normal, I mean, we were going to be late, but it wasn't that dramatic..... BUT THE REST WAS HORRIBLE. The flight was fine, but it was getting very tiring. I flew with Emirates, and let me tell you, it was incredible. The 15-hour Canadian air flight was becoming tedious because I had almost watched 5 different movies and had nothing else to do.

Then we had to go get our bags, but I was so tired that I couldn't even remember my own name. We arrived at our apartment, made a few adjustments, and fell asleep.

We'll start properly setting up later, but first we have to unpack.

Anyway if you made it till here......THANKS BRUV ✪ ω ✪
